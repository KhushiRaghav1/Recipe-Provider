Mango Shake Recipe



Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe mangoes, peeled and chopped
  • 2 cups of milk (chilled)
  • 1 cup of ice cubes
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar (optional)
  • A pinch of cardamom powder (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Place the chopped mangoes in a blender.
  2. Add the chilled milk, ice cubes, and sugar.
  3. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  4. Taste and add more sugar if needed. Optionally, add a pinch of cardamom powder for extra flavor.
  5. Pour the mango shake into glasses and serve immediately.

Tools/Equipment

Tool Description
Blender For blending the ingredients together.
Knife For chopping the mangoes.
Cutting Board To safely chop the mangoes.
Measuring Cups To measure the ingredients accurately.

Cold Coffee Recipe




Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of chilled milk
  • 1 tablespoon of instant coffee powder
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 1/4 cup of water
  • 2-3 ice cubes
  • Vanilla ice cream or whipped cream (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, mix the coffee powder and sugar with the water until fully dissolved.
  2. Pour the coffee mixture into a blender.
  3. Add the chilled milk and ice cubes.
  4. Blend until smooth and frothy.
  5. Pour the cold coffee into a tall glass.
  6. Top with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream if desired.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Tools/Equipment

Tool Description
Blender For blending the coffee mixture.
Measuring Spoons To measure the coffee powder and sugar accurately.
Measuring Cup To measure the milk.
Glass To serve the cold coffee.
